Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of flat, wide pasta sheets, often alternating with rich fillings like ground meat (typically beef or pork), tomato sauce, and a creamy béchamel or ricotta cheese mixture. Each layer builds up a hearty, flavorful combination, often enhanced with Italian herbs like basil, oregano, and garlic. The top is finished with a generous sprinkle of mozzarella or Parmesan cheese, which melts and browns to create a deliciously golden crust when baked. Lasagna is known for its comforting, cheesy, and savory flavor profile, making it a favorite at gatherings and family dinners.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage
  • 3/4 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans canned tomato sauce
  • 2 (6 ounce) cans tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup water
  • ½ cup water
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 4 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley, divided
  • 1 ½ teaspoons dried basil leaves
  • 1 ½ teaspoons salt, divided, or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • ½ teaspoon fennel seeds
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • ¾ pound mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • ¾ cup grated Parmesan cheese
